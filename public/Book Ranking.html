The Best Books According to Jayson

My top 3 books of all time

Lie with me, by Philippe Besson

One of the most heartbreaking queer fiction I have ever read. The melancholy, the sweet taste of an unrealized love, forbidden by social prejudice of the 80s. And suicide, let's not talk much about it.

The Psychology of money, by Morgan Housel

This book will change your perspective of money, through lessons learned from different individuals with different backgrounds.

Reasons to stay alive, by Matt Haig

When life is down and you feel blue, read this book.
